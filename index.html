// ABOUT

No Signal are a four-piece rock covers band from Bribie Island, Queensland — here to make some noise and have a damn good time doing it. Formed by four mates who share a simple belief: great rock music never gets old.

Drawing from five decades of rock and everything in its orbit, the set list spans classic rock, hard rock, pub rock, and anything else that hits hard and sounds massive live. If it rocks, it's fair game.

No Signal are available for pubs, clubs, private events, and anywhere else that appreciates a band that turns up loud and delivers the goods.
